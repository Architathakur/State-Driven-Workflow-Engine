REAL-LIFE SCENARIO

Company Policy Approval Pipeline

Situation:

An employee writes a company policy document.
It must go through:

- Drafting
- Manager review
- Approval / rejection
- Possible rework
- Final approval

Mapping Real Life -> Code

Real Life Action  ->	Code Call
Employee drafts policy	new Document()
Employee edits policy	document.edit()
Employee submits policy	document.submit()
Manager reviews policy	(implicit – state = REVIEW)
Manager rejects policy	document.reject()
Employee revises policy	document.edit()
Employee resubmits	document.submit()
Manager approves	document.approve()
Policy is locked	ApprovedState
